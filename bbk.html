Basic Business Knowledge & Total Credits

Understanding your MSIS curriculum and credit requirements

What is BBK?

Basic Business Knowledge (BBK) courses provide essential business foundations for students who may not have an undergraduate business background. These courses ensure all MSIS students have the core business competencies needed to succeed in the program.

The total credit requirement for the MSIS degree varies based on your background. Students with business degrees may need fewer BBK courses, while those from non-business backgrounds may need additional foundational courses.

Basic Business Knowledge (BBK) Courses

Foundational courses for students without a business background

BBK

Accounting Fundamentals

3 Credits

Introduction to financial and managerial accounting principles, financial statements, and business decision-making.

BBK

Finance Fundamentals

3 Credits

Basic concepts of corporate finance, investment analysis, risk management, and financial markets.

BBK

Marketing Fundamentals

3 Credits

Core marketing concepts, consumer behavior, market research, and strategic marketing planning.

BBK

Operations Management

3 Credits

Process management, supply chain operations, quality control, and operational efficiency.

Note: BBK requirements are determined during your initial advising session. Not all students need all BBK courses - it depends on your undergraduate coursework.
